**INTRODUCTION**

In today's fast-changing digital world, students are expected not only to use technology but also to understand it responsibly. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I see my role as someone who can guide others in using digital tools wisely while maintaining a safe and professional online presence. This project gave me a practical opportunity to explore these aspects in a structured way.

By completing the five modules, I worked on creating digital content, building professional profiles, improving communication skills, and understanding cyber safety. Each task was not just an assignment but a learning experience that helped me become more confident in navigating the digital environment. Overall, this project strengthened my awareness of how technology can be used effectively for both academic growth and personal development.

**TASK 1: DIGITAL LITERACY AWARENESS INFOGRAPHIC**

For this task, I used Canva to create a digital literacy infographic that highlights the importance of being aware and responsible while using digital technologies. In my design, I covered topics such as what is digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence.

While designing the infographic, I focused on making it visually clear and easy to understand. I used simple language, relevant icons, and balanced colors so that the information looks attractive and meaningful at the same time. One of the main challenges I faced was arranging all the important points in a limited space without making the design look crowded.

The most interesting part of this task was learning how to present information creatively rather than just writing it in paragraphs. Overall, this activity improved my understanding of visual communication and showed me how tools like Canva can be effectively used to share ideas.

**TASK 2:** **Student Digital Portfolio**

In this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build a strong digital presence. These platforms are widely used by students and professionals to showcase their skills, projects, and achievements. GitHub allows me to upload and manage my projects, LinkedIn helps in building professional connections, and Kaggle provides a platform to explore data science and improve problem-solving skills.

While setting up these profiles, I ensured that the information was clear, professional, and relevant to my academic background. This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a clean and organized online identity.

Over the next four years, I plan to regularly update these platforms by adding new projects, learning new skills, and engaging with online communities. This will not only help me track my progress but also improve my chances of securing internships and job opportunities in the future.

**TASK 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms**

For this task, I explored both coding practice and collaboration tools. In the first part, I created an account on HackerRank and completed a beginner-level coding challenge. This activity helped me understand the basics of problem-solving and logical thinking. Even though the question was simple, it required careful understanding and proper implementation, which made the experience meaningful.

In the second part, I created a Google Form as a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz. The form included five questions with a mix of multiple-choice and short-answer types. I also learned how responses are automatically collected and organized in Google Sheets, which makes data handling easier.

This task showed me how coding platforms can improve technical skills, while tools like Google Forms can be used for collaboration and data collection. Overall, it was a useful experience that helped me understand the practical applications of these platforms in academics.

**TASK 4: Email Etiquette & Communication**

This task helped me understand the importance of professional communication in academic and professional life. I wrote two formal emails-one to a professor requesting an extension and another to an internship coordinator expressing interest in an opportunity. While writing these emails, I focused on using clear subject lines, polite language, and a proper structure.

I also learned that poor communication can create confusion and leave a negative impression. For example, if a student sends an email without a clear subject or uses informal language, the message may be ignored or misunderstood. Such situations can lead to delays or missed opportunities.

Through this task, I realized that effective communication is not just about what we say, but also how we present it. It improved my confidence in writing professional emails.

**Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness**

In this task, I explored different types of cybercrimes, especially phishing and online payment fraud. What surprised me the most was how easily people can become victims due to small mistakes, such as clicking on unknown links or sharing personal information without verification. It made me realize that lack of awareness is one of the biggest reasons behind such incidents.

I also created a prevention checklist with simple and practical steps to stay safe online. This task helped me understand the importance of being cautious while using digital platforms.

After completing this task, I have decided to be more careful while handling online transactions and avoid trusting unknown sources. Overall, it increased my awareness and encouraged me to adopt safer digital habits in my daily life.

**Conclusion**

This project has been a truly enriching experience that helped me understand the real importance of digital literacy in today's technology-driven world. Through the various tasks, I learned how to use digital tools effectively, build a professional online presence, and communicate in a clear and responsible manner. I also developed a better understanding of cyber safety and the importance of staying alert while using online platforms.

Overall, this project has increased my confidence in using technology and encouraged me to adopt safe and smart digital practices that will support my academic growth and future career.
